A hand-bound multi-media book of thoughts, materials, and found objects.
This book explores the highly ephemeral yet deeply meaningful relationships one forms throughout their life. It is mostly inspired by young queer love, and also ties in the instability of a defined home/family/community while in college. It is hopeless and hopeful, wishing for things to stay the same but also accepting that change will happen no matter what, and that only what is now present can be loved. It's about spring, rebirth, and reliving the present in its endless forms over and over again, forever until we die.
