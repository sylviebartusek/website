Lighting an Outdoor Scene
4/12/23
Autodesk Maya
I created this image using given shaded models, by aranging them into a dynamic scene and experimenting with the lighting and the camera. The focus of this study was composition and lighting.

My vision was for this shot to be from the perspective of someone getting off a trolley to walk into the Crown Hotel. The focus of the shot is the hotel's sign. I chose a somewhat medium shot with a focal length of 40 to put the camera in the perspective of the viewer. I wanted the sky to look overcast, maybe even a little rainy or right before a thunderstorm. The time is about 5pm, before it really starts getting dark.
