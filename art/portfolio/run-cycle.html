A short animated film I worked on with 3 other students in my Intro to Computer Animation class.
Pre-production:
  • Helped create the shotlist and storyboarded some scenes.
Production:
  • Worked in the 3D animation software Maya.
  • Modeled and rigged two horse heads, and modeled environmental assets
  • Animated and rendered a good portion of the shots. Most animation done by me was environmental (carousel mainly).
Post-production:
  • Edited a good amount of the film in Adobe Premiere, specifically the scenes before the more experimental section, and the scenes that show the final resolution. This included Color correction, covering up glitches in the rendered shots, and fixing timing problems.
  • Led my team as far as the film's soundtrack. I did most of the experimenting, recording, and editing with the soundtrack. The recordings were all performed by myself as well as other student musicians I invited to contribute to the project. I was in charge of all the sound engineering in my dorm room, which I converted into a small makeshift studio.
