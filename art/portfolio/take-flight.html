Take Flight
5/10/24
Autodesk Maya, Advanced Skeleton Plugin, MEL Scripting
This is a short reel, showcasing my body and facial rig work for the short film Take Flight. I created the facial rigs of the two main characters of the film, a father and his daughter, using Maya's Advanced Skeleton Plugin. Additionally, I created the daughter's body rig from scratch.

This film is still in production. However, a current preview is available on vimeo under this link. The password is fixpermits24.
As the film is still being worked on, I am taking on environment simulation work, and reworking the dad character's body rig.
