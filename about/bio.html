bio resume
sylvie bartusek is an artist, musician, and technologist from silver spring, maryland.
i have recently graduated from brown university, where i received a bachelor of science for my studies in computer science and visual art.

i like to think about technology, time, community, books, bodies, sounds, experiments, people, and messes.

i spend my free time playing the guitar, drums, and bass with anyone who will play along. I love to collect discarded materials to make into sculptures thinking about multi-sensory interactions. art is for everyone! let's make something together.
